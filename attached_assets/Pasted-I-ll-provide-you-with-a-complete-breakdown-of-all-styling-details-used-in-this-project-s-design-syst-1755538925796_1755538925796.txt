## **Animations & Effects**

### **Keyframe Animations**
1. **Breathe**: Scale animation (1 → 1.02 → 1) with glow effect
2. **Particle Float**: Floating particles with rotation (0° → 180°)
3. **Typewriter**: Width animation for text reveal
4. **Glow Pulse**: Box-shadow pulsing effect
5. **Fade In**: Opacity + translateY animation
6. **Accordion**: Built-in Radix accordion animations

### **Animation Durations**
- **Breathe**: 4s infinite
- **Particle Float**: 3s infinite
- **Typewriter**: 2s
- **Glow Pulse**: 2s infinite
- **Fade In**: 0.3s
- **Accordion**: 0.2s

### **Transition Effects**
- **Standard Transition**: `all 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`
- **Hover Effects**: 200ms duration
- **Transform Effects**: 300ms duration
